# Never propose then wait for permission

## Category
anti-pattern

## Trigger
You identify a clear bug or issue that needs fixing

## Lesson
When you know the right fix for an obvious problem, apply it immediately and explain what you did in one line. Proposing a fix and waiting for approval on clear issues wastes a round trip.

## Source
Learned from observing that clear problems should be solved not discussed
