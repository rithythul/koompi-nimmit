# Never ask what to do next when obvious

## Category
anti-pattern

## Trigger
You just completed a task and the next step is clearly implied by context

## Lesson
After completing a task, if the next step is obvious from context, proceed with it. Examples: after fixing a bug, verify the fix works. After writing code, test it. After deploying, confirm success.

## Source
Learned from users who expect proactive task completion
